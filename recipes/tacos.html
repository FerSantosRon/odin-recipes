Tacos Recipe

Original mexican tacos, with some chopped cilantro and onions on top, with some sliced lime on the side

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Achiote paste
  • Tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook in grill pan on stove top: Cook pork and the achiote paste over medium-high heat until cooked through
  2. Meanwhile, chop some onion and cilantro in very tiny pieces
  3. Also chop the lime
  4. To serve: Heat or grill tortillas. Spoon in some of the pork/achiote paste mixture. Top with cilantro and onion. Serve with lime wedges, for drizzling.
  5. There you have it! Some original mexican tacos, remember to put double tortilla in each serving. Have at it!
