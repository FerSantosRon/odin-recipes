Sandwich Recipe

The best sandwich that you can have, simple but delicious, and with very few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Take 2 pieces of bread and spread some mayo in them
  2. In between the 2 pieces of bread, put the ingredients as follows: 1 slice of american cheese, 2 slices of ham, some lettuce, and a slice of tomato
  3. Put it all together, toast the sandwich 2 minutes per side
  4. Finally on a plate and start eating!
