Chillidog's Recipe

American classic chili dog, grilled hot dog on a bun, smothered in ground beef chili sauce, sprinkled with cheddar cheese and onions.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Hot dogs
  • Ground beef
  • Chilli sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Onions

Steps

  1. In a pan, Stir in the ground beef. Cook over high heat, stirring occasionally, until the beef is browned, a few minutes.
  2. Once the beef is well browned, stir in the chilli sauce.
  3. Cook the chili on a gentle simmer for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Grill the hot dogs over medium heat until they get a light char. Grill the hot dog buns briefly if you want, no more than a minute.
  5. Dog goes in bun, chili goes on top, sprinkle on chopped onion and shredded cheese, and have at it!
